Controls:
Accelerate Key: Right Arrow
Backwards Move Key: Left Arrow
Break Key: B
Horn: H

Basic Info:
There are 4 lives available for each level. Try to get to the 
end within the time limit without violating the highway code.

Before attempting to play, see the information next to each 
level button.

Score in each level more than 20 and 30 to unlock the yellow 
and police car, respectively.
